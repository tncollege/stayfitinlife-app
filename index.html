Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

Stayfitinlife respects your privacy. This app may store personal wellness information such as your name, age, body measurements, weight entries, workout logs, meal logs, hydration records, supplement tracking, and recovery-related inputs.

In the current version of the app, your data is stored locally on your device/browser unless you manually export and share it.

Stayfitinlife does not sell your data and does not intentionally share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

If cloud sync, login, integrations, or analytics are added in future versions, this policy will be updated to reflect those changes.

For privacy-related questions or support, contact support@stayfitinlife.com.
