# Privacy Policy

**Version:** Stable Version 1.0  
**Last Updated:** April 25, 2026

STAYFITINLIFE stores your profile, nutrition, workout, recovery, progress and coaching data to provide fitness tracking and planning features.

Your data is used to calculate targets, generate local recommendations, show progress, and support AI Coach responses when you choose to use AI features.

AI Coach requests may be processed by an external AI provider only when you actively ask the AI Coach a question. The app includes local logic, caching, and usage limits to reduce unnecessary AI processing.

Do not enter sensitive medical, financial, or emergency information. STAYFITINLIFE is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis.

You are responsible for reviewing your data and using the app safely.
